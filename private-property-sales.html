Private Property Sales


Selecting good rent paying tenants and proper maintenance of various classes of built-up properties is one of the best ways by which real estate investment holdings will continue to appreciate both in monetary and aesthetic values.

Shelter Trust been a real estate management professional expert is readily at your disposal if and when contacted or consulted by any property owner/investor to offer our services on issues relating to the management of the landed properties (land and buildings) to the highest and best use.

Our roles as a company while rendering property management services to clients involves:

  1. Identify and select good rent paying tenants
  2. Run a background check on all prospective new tenants
  3. Preparation of a lease agreement document for both tenant and landlord
  4. Market and advertise client’s landed property to prospective tenants
  5. Carry out a schedule of property condition on client’s property prior to tenants moving in
  6. Carry out a schedule of property dilapidation prior to the tenant moving out
  7. Advise property owner on the current/actual rental value of their property
  8. Supervision of all minor and major remedial works on the client’s property been managed
  9. Act as property manager/management surveyor for the property owner
  10. Represent the beneficial interests of the property owner.
